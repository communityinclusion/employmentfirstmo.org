Building on Success: Expanding Employment Opportunities

November 30, 2016

Best Western Royal Plaza, 181 Boston Post Road, West Marlborough, MA 01752

8:30AM–9:30AM: Registration, exhibits, and networking
9:30AM–4:00PM: Conference, including keynote, panel discussions, breakout sessions, and more

Join us for this conference, designed for Massachusetts Department of Developmental Services (DDS) staff and providers, educators, individuals with disabilities, and family members, as well as other state agency partners.

What's it all about?

At this day-long event, we’ll celebrate what’s been accomplished in Massachusetts in terms of integrated employment. A variety of sessions will provide information and resources to support the continued expansion of employment opportunities for individuals served by DDS.

This event is presented by the Institute for Community Inclusion (ICI) in partnership with DDS. You'll have access to presentations by key state leaders, a panel discussion, exhibit tables, and relevant and innovative breakout sessions. Stay tuned for updates on speakers and session topics as the program develops.

This conference is sure to energize your commitment and give you practical material you can use to achieve successful employment outcomes!

Call for presentations

The ICI, in partnership with DDS, is seeking proposals for breakout session presentations. Proposals should display innovative and community-based practices that promote integrated employment outcomes for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities.

Learn more and submit your proposal.

Proposals are due on September 29 by 5:00 PM EDT.
