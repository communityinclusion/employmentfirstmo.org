--- layout: reg_layout title: Transition to Community-Based Work and Non Work Services - What Does It Mean to You? ---

Implementation of the MA Blueprint for Success will involve transitions for staff who have typically provided services in center-based environments. This introductory training provides center-based staff with an overview of the emerging service environment as it relates to the ongoing transformation to competitive work and community activity, and the implications for their work.

Participants will explore the values shift that is underway and learn about promising practices in community-based work and non-work services. We will also discuss ways to engage individuals, family members, and other stakeholders. The training will include structured group activities along with facilitated discussion.

Participants will:

  • Learn about the emerging service environment for people with disabilities in MA
  • Explore core values that drive person-centered, inclusive services
  • Become familiar with promising practices in work and non-work services
  • Gain strategies to work effectively with individuals and family members during this transition
