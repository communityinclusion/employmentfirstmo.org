--- layout: reg_layout title: Managing for Success ---

This two-day training session provides employment service managers and supervisors with the knowledge and skills needed to lead successful community-based employment programs.

Participants will:

  • Explore their supervisory style and understand its impact in supporting employees.
  • Improve their communication and conflict resolution skills.
  • Learn how to build motivated and productive teams.
  • Identify program goals and analyze best practices for achieving them.
  • Establish efficient and timely approaches for measuring program results.
