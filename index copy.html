A "promising practice" is an innovative policy, practice, or procedure that promotes integrated community employment for individuals with intellectual disabilities.

This series of promising practices illustrates creative strategies that promote Employment First in Massachusetts. We are sharing them here to spread the word and spark the imagination of service providers, individuals with disabilities, and their families.
