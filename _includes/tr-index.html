The Employment First Missouri Community of Practice

This community features:

  • Webinars providing content focused on organizational transformation, best practices in employment services, and facilitated discussion with participants. Webinars occur monthly and last for one hour. Automatic captions will be available. For accommodation requests, email Jessica.keenoy@umb.edu. All web sessions are offered at no cost and certificates of attendance are available for sessions attended live.
  • Online Basecamp Community offers an ongoing interactive forum to connect with other service providers, share resources, and celebrate successes. Archived webinars are available on Basecamp. There are currently over 250 members in the Basecamp Community. Email Jessica.keenoy@umb.edu to be added to this community.
